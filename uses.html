Uses

Commercial





Robots have many functions that can be used in a work
setting. Since the robot is programmed with an internal
GPS, it can serve as a taxi driver of help with any map
related issues. Furthermore, robots can take the place
of humans in dangerous jobs such as construction work.
The benefit of having a robot is the one time cost
and a few maintenance cost rather than a yearly
salary which could potentially
benefit a company.

Recreational




Utilibot has many features that provides entertainment to users. The robot
is capable of playing games such as chess or checkers. Furthermore,
the robot can share stories as well as make videos.






Safety





Robots, as advanced as modern technology brings us, are still machines and can malfunction which results in dangerous results. People need to be wary about the possibility of the robots system having a reaction from the circuits or any other malfunction.
